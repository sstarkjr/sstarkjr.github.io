## Alumni Panel — From Academia to Industry

**University of Chicago, MS in Applied Data Science · March 23, 2024**

Invited as an alumni panelist for the MS in Applied Data Science Immersion Weekend at the Rubenstein Forum, University of Chicago. The panel was facilitated by Dr. Arnab Bose and focused on how graduates apply their academic training to real-world industry roles.

Topics covered:
- Which techniques and concepts from the program translate most directly to industry work
- What program attributes had the most lasting impact
- Advice for incoming and current students navigating the transition from academia to industry

*One of three alumni panelists representing industry data science roles.*
